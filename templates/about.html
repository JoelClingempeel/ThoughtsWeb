About

The web of thoughts allows you to take notes in the form of an interactive graph with the options of organizing nodes hierarchically and of appending various (text-based) notes to nodes. Through exploring the graph you can also discover similar thoughts of other users (if they have opted to make the corresponding parts of their graphs public).

To get started, create an account and head over to the graph page to begin exploring. You can also check out trending to see what is currently popular. {% endblock %}
